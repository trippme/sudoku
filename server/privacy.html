Sudoku — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

This Sudoku app lets you play locally and, optionally, compete with friends. Most of the app works entirely on your device. This policy explains the limited data the optional online features use.

What we collect

Only if you choose to use the online features (leaderboard, sending games to friends, competing) and enter a profile:

  • Display name and email address that you enter. Your email is your identity — there is no password. Friends you add can see the email you used (that's how they compete with you); your email is not shown on the global leaderboard (only your display name is).
  • Game results you finish: the game number, your time, and how many hints/mistakes you used.
  • Friends' email addresses that you add, so you can send them games and results.

If you never set a profile, the app sends nothing to our server — your games, settings, and statistics stay on your device.

How it's used

Solely to provide the features you ask for: showing leaderboards, letting you send a game to a friend's in‑app inbox, and notifying competitors of each other's results. We do not use it for advertising or profiling.

Where it's stored

On‑device data (saved games, settings, statistics, your profile) is stored locally. Online data (the items above) is stored on our server at the949dude.com and transmitted over HTTPS. We do not sell or share your data with third parties, and the app contains no third‑party advertising or analytics trackers.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Data retention and deletion

You can stop sending data at any time by not using the online features. To have your stored results, profile, and shares deleted, email us and we'll remove all records associated with your email address.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests: privacy@the949dude.com
